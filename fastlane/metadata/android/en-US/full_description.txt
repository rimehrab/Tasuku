<p><b>Tasuku</b> is a minimalist Android to-do application built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. It follows the "Material Expressive" design, prioritizing a clean, modern interface and a seamless user experience.</p><p>Key features:</p><ul><li><b>Material 3 Expressive design:</b> Sleek and modern UI with fluid transitions and card-based layouts.</li><li><b>Dynamic color:</b> Seamlessly adapts to your wallpaper's color scheme on Android 12+ via Material You.</li><li><b>Theming modes:</b> Full support for Light, Dark, and System (auto-switching) themes.</li><li><b>Focused workflow:</b> Efficient task entry and management designed to stay out of your way.</li><li><b>Offline-first:</b> All tasks are stored locally via Room Database — no accounts or network required.</li></ul>
